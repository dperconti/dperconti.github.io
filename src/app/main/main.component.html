Donato Perconti

Entrepeneur, technologist, staff engineer
Donato Perconti is an entrepreneurial technologist that enjoys solving complex business problems with software.

Donato is currently a staff software engineer for the educational technology company Skillshare, where he is tasked with intersecting big data, payments, and finance. Previously, Donato worked with numerous companies ranging from lululemon, Thinkful, Airship, and intel. As a former founder at Paylasso, Donato is constantly vetting and exploring new business models and ideas to create new companies.




Growth Mindset

Donato believes business solutions can change the world for the better - smart and revenue rich while also making a social impact. He also believes in the power of a growth mindset. One outlet of Donato's growth-mindset is the MOOC (Massive Open Online Courses) powered curriculum for business mastership: MOOC MBA. Donato also holds multiple certificates in various subjects, including entrepreneurship, AWS technologies, and data science.

Donato is an alumnus of Lewis & Clark College, where he studied mathematics and computer science. He is also active in various community organizations such as Little League Baseball and Softball, local school boards, and Portland's Night Strike homeless ministry.
